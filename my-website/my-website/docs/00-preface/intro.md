## Introduction to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

Welcome to the "Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics" textbook. This book serves as a comprehensive guide for understanding and developing intelligent humanoid robots capable of interacting with the physical world. It covers fundamental concepts, practical implementations using ROS 2 and NVIDIA Isaac, and advanced topics in vision-language-action models and conversational AI.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, providing readers with the tools and insights needed to contribute to the exciting field of physical AI. Whether you are a student, researcher, or enthusiast, this textbook offers a structured learning path to master the complexities of embodied intelligence and humanoid robotics.

### Key Learning Objectives
* Understand the core concepts of physical AI and embodied intelligence.
* Gain proficiency in ROS 2 for robot control and communication.
* Learn to create and simulate digital twins in Gazebo and Unity.
* Explore the NVIDIA Isaac platform for advanced robotics development.
* Delve into vision-language-action models for intelligent robot behavior.
* Develop skills in humanoid kinematics, dynamics, and bipedal locomotion.
* Integrate conversational AI for natural human-robot interaction.
* Identify essential hardware for physical AI development.

### Summary
This introduction provides an overview of the textbook's scope and objectives. It sets the stage for a deep dive into the principles and practices of building intelligent humanoid robots, emphasizing both theoretical understanding and practical implementation.
