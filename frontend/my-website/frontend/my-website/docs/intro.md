## Course Overview

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics, covering fundamental concepts, cutting-edge technologies, and practical applications. Students will gain hands-on experience with industry-standard tools and platforms, including ROS2, Gazebo, Unity, NVIDIA Isaac Sim, and Visual-Language-Action (VLA) models. The curriculum is designed to equip learners with the skills necessary to design, develop, and deploy intelligent robotic systems in various real-world scenarios.

<PersonalizedContent difficulty="beginner">

<PersonalizedContent difficulty="advanced">
